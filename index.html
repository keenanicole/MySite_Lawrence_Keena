What is a Brony?

Typically refers to 13-30 year old male fans of the 2010 reboot of the show My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, but may also include females.

Brony = Bro + Pony

While generally associated with a negative stereotype by outsiders, due to it's former 80's frilly girly-girl twinkle-toed tea-parties and all-female main casting, bronies are attracted to the new show by it's good animation, acting, writing, and humor. Having once been on the outside, bronies can love and tolerate others until they themselves become bronies. However, the adult fandom of My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic has led to references to the program in popular culture, such as secret levels on Diablo III and Saturday Night Live spoofs.

How To Become A Brony

  1. Watch the show. Just watch it.
  2. Stay informed. Always know when the next episode is going to air and always know what is going on in the brony community
  3. Meet other Bronies. You need friends who understand your pony love, to watch the newest episode with or even have a Brony party!
  4. Get pony merch. Stock up on DVD's, t-shirts and figurines.
  5. Dress up and head out to a Brony Convention.
  6. Be proud to be a Brony! There is nothing wrong with being a 30 something male with love for a children's show about friendship and love featuring sparkling rainbow ponies!
